About Us

Horticulture Management System provides its users and researches to get online information about, the crops and other horticulture products. The page contains user registration page where they can enter their requirement and have option to buy perticular product like saplings, fruits, herbs etc. Admin login allows to view the customer details and remove customers. As the number is rising there is a need of effective management of Crop Details, one such effective system is our Horticulture Management System its designed using HTML and CSS for frontend and PHP as backend database. The actions like login, register, add, delete, payment are provided. The Horticulture Crop Management System stores the details like Customer_name, password, requirements, Phone No, Email, of the users who visits.
